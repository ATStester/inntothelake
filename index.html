Our Suites

At Inn to the Lake, we offer five beautifully appointed suites, each designed to provide a unique and comfortable experience for every guest.

  • The Lakeview Master Suite: Experience the luxurious comfort of a spacious king-size bed with an attached full bath, offering breathtaking views of Lake Skaha. Perfect for those seeking a private and relaxing retreat.
  • The Vineyard Suite: A cozy, elegant room with a peaceful ambiance, offering scenic views of the surrounding vineyards and the Okanagan Valley. Ideal for couples or those looking for a serene escape.
  • The Skaha Suite: Inspired by the natural beauty of Lake Skaha, this suite features calming decor and tranquil surroundings, perfect for those looking to unwind and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of the area.
  • The Sunrise Suite: A bright and airy room with stunning views to the east, making it the perfect choice for morning risers who want to bask in the first rays of sunlight and enjoy the fresh Okanagan mornings.
  • The Orchard Suite: Embracing the natural beauty of the Okanagan, this suite is inspired by the local orchards and features earthy tones and organic elements, offering a peaceful and refreshing environment for a relaxing stay.

Things to Do at Inn to the Lake

Located in the heart of the Okanagan Valley, Inn to the Lake offers a perfect base to explore the stunning natural beauty and diverse activities of the area.

  • Outdoor Recreation: Enjoy swimming, kayaking, and paddleboarding on Lake Skaha, or explore the Kettle Valley Rail Trail and Skaha Bluffs for hiking and mountain biking.
  • Wine & Craft Beer: Experience the local wine tours and visit Penticton’s renowned craft breweries for a taste of the region’s finest beverages.
  • Family Fun: Relax on nearby sandy beaches, float along the Okanagan Channel, or explore the Penticton Farmers Market for fresh local produce and handmade goods.
  • Winter Activities (Seasonal): Ski, snowboard, or snowshoe at Apex Mountain Resort, just a short drive away.

Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, Inn to the Lake is the ideal starting point to enjoy all that Penticton has to offer.
